GAIL Polymer Sales Playbook

Positioning Strategy

  • Recommend based on application fit, not price
  • Highlight consistency and processing advantages
  • Leverage plant technology credibility

Technology Strength

  • HDPE: Mitsui CX Slurry (Japan)
  • LLDPE: Unipol / Sclairtech
  • PP: Unipol (WR Grace)
  • PTA: INEOS (UK)

Customer Validation Questions

  • What is your processing method?
  • Do you prioritize toughness or processability?
  • Is application indoor or outdoor?
  • What is your machine type and output?

Risk Awareness

  • High MI → Lower strength
  • Low MI → Harder processing
  • Incorrect density → performance issues

Fallback Strategy

  • If no exact match → recommend closest grade
  • Suggest trial validation
  • Escalate to GPTC if required
